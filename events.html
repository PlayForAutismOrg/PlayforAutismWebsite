2024 Recap — 2nd Annual Swing for Autism

A look back at our golf outing held August 3, 2024 at Bruce Hills Golf Club.

Swing for Autism — 2nd Annual Golf Outing

  • Date: August 3, 2024
  • Location: Bruce Hills Golf Club, 6771 Taft Rd, Bruce Township, MI 48065
  • Format: 18 holes with cart, 8:30 AM shotgun start

What was included

  • Welcome gift for every golfer
  • Lunch on the turn & dinner after the round
  • Drink tickets and cash bar
  • Hole-in-One contest — chance to win a vehicle & $50,000 (half to charity)

Sponsorship tiers offered

Eagle $3,000 Birdie $2,500 Par $1,500 Ball $1,000 Mini Game $500 Hole $200
