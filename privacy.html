Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Who We Are

Play for Autism Foundation ("we," "us," or "our") is a nonprofit organization based in Chesterfield, Michigan. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit playforautism.org.

Information We Collect

Contact Form Submissions

When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message. This information is processed by Web3Forms and delivered to our team email. We do not store form submissions on our website.

Purchases and Donations

All payments are processed by Square on their hosted checkout pages. We never collect, see, or store your credit card number, billing address, or other payment details on this website. Square's handling of your payment information is governed by Square's Privacy Policy.

Shopping Cart

Your shopping cart is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is cleared when you complete checkout or clear your browser data.

Service Worker Caching

Our website uses a service worker to cache pages and assets for faster loading and limited offline access. Cached data is stored only in your browser and can be cleared through your browser settings.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.
  • We do not use Google Analytics or any analytics platform.
  • We do not use advertising pixels or retargeting.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our website relies on the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages — hosts our website files. GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Cloudflare — provides DNS, CDN, and security services. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Square — processes payments and donations. Square Privacy Policy
  • Web3Forms — delivers contact form submissions. Web3Forms Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry. Purchase records are maintained by Square in accordance with their data retention policies and applicable tax and accounting requirements.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal information we hold about you by contacting us at admin@playforautismfoundation.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our contact form, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
