Our mission

Play for Autism (PFA) Foundation is a nonprofit dedicated to supporting children and families affected by autism spectrum disorder. Based in Southeast Michigan, we raise awareness and funds through community events like our annual Swing for Autism golf outing.

Our values

  • Community-driven fundraising
  • 100% of proceeds to families
  • Inclusive, family-friendly events
  • Local partnerships and sponsorships
  • Transparency and accountability

What we do

We organize the annual Swing for Autism (SFA) golf outing at Bruce Hills Golf Club in Bruce Township, Michigan. The event brings together golfers, sponsors, and community members to raise money for autism support programs.

Beyond golf, we work with local vendors and sponsors to build a network of support — from donated goods and services to direct financial contributions that go to families in need.

Funding comes from event registration, sponsorships, merchandise sales, and direct donations.

Who we serve

Families, children, and caregivers across Southeast Michigan who are navigating life on the autism spectrum. Whether through direct financial assistance, community events, or resource connections, PFA is here to help.

Founded by Tristan and Nicole Parrish out of Chesterfield, Michigan.
